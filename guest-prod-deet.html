"Blue Spring hinges" in eyeglasses typically refer to a type of hinge mechanism that allows the temples (arms) of the glasses to flex outward when pressure is applied and then return to their original position. This design is often used for added durability and comfort, as it can provide a better fit for a wider range of head sizes and shapes.
